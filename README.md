# Carbon Sequestration Prediction: Zhumadian City

This project focuses on predicting carbon sequestration capacity for **Zhumadian City**. It utilizes monthly environmental data from **December 2023 to May 2024**, processed via Google Earth Engine (GEE), to establish a robust predictive model.

## Project Structure
- `gee_download.js`: GEE script for acquiring and pre-processing monthly time-series data for the Zhumadian study area.
- `README.md`: Documentation on the research methodology and data sources.

## Study Area & Data
- **Study Area**: Zhumadian City, China.
- **Temporal Scope**: Monthly time-series data from December 2023 to May 2024.
- **Dataset Description**: The study utilizes monthly carbon sequestration estimation results as the core input for predictive modeling.
- **Data Availability**: The processed monthly datasets for Zhumadian (Dec 2023 - May 2024) are publicly available here: [Download Zhumadian Carbon Sequestration Datasets](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1vsYt3tHwH80UmcVFetNktR9yEnk6wTWB/view?usp=sharing)

## Research Objectives
1. **Time-series Analysis**: Analyzing the monthly trends and variations of carbon sequestration patterns in Zhumadian City.
2. **Predictive Modeling**: Developing a model to estimate carbon sequestration potential based on the specified six-month observation period.
3. **Application**: Providing localized, data-driven insights for carbon cycle management and agricultural monitoring.
